About Us

Welcome to Mia Valentina, where dining is not just a meal, but a culinary experience. Our restaurant is the result of a deep love for Italian cuisine, driven by our passion to serve exquisite flavors create unforgettable moments, and offer exceptional hospitality.


We offer a delectable range of dishes that will transport your taste buds straight to Italy. Our menu features a variety of classic Italian dishes, from pasta to pizza and tiramisu, all made with the freshest ingredients and prepared by our talented chefs with years of experience.


Our warm and inviting atmosphere will make you feel right at home, and our friendly staff is always happy to make recommendations or help you select the perfect wine to complement your meal. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner for two or a family celebration, Mia Valentina is the perfect destination for a memorable Italian dining experience. Come and join us for a taste of Italy right in the heart of Budapest.


We believe that dining is an art form, and our restaurant is designed to provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere that will transport you to a world of culinary pleasure. Whether you're here to celebrate a special occasion, enjoy a romantic dinner for two, or simply savor a meal with family and friends, our restaurant offers an unforgettable setting where you can relax, let go, and create lasting memories.


Thank you for choosing Mia Valentina. We look forward to welcoming you to our restaurant and creating a culinary experience that will leave you with unforgettable memories and an irresistible craving to return. Buon appetito!
